About Me

Let my experience and extensive research into all things Walt Disney World make your wishes come true. There is an infinite amount of magic available at Walt Disney World and I want you to experience it all. From the perfect seats for the Wishes fireworks show, down to the simplicity of having your luggage magically arrive in your room from the airport.

My passion for Disney started during my first trip to Walt Disney World with my wife. Since then we have visited every other year, even with the addition of our own two little princesses. Every vacation has been a unique experience and as the children get older, we discover Disney all over again through their eyes and it always remains as breathtaking.

Whether you have yet to experience Disney Magic or simply don't want to do all the planning yourself I am ready to plan as little or as much of your vacation with you.
